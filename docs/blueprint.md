# **App Name**: Babdodo Explorer

## Core Features:

- Tour, Safari and Transfer Listings: Display tours, safaris, and transfer services with details such as title, description, price, duration, location, images, and included/excluded services.
- Admin Dashboard with CRUD Operations: Enable administrators to Create, Read, Update, and Delete tours, safaris, transfers, and blog posts, as well as manage images, prices, descriptions, and availability via a secure and intuitive admin dashboard.
- Image management: Allow administrators to upload and manage images associated with tours, safaris, and transfers. Optimize images for web to ensure fast loading times and good performance.
- Content Recommendations: AI-powered tool to suggest related blog posts or other tour options to users based on their current selection or browsing history to increase engagement and discovery.
- Contact Form: Enable a contact form for users to submit inquiries. Ensure the form is connected to Supabase for storing submissions or forwarding to a designated email address.
- WhatsApp Integration: Integrate a WhatsApp contact button or chat functionality to enable instant communication with potential customers, enhancing user experience and customer support.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A warm sand tone (#F4EBCD) evoking the beaches of Zanzibar and the landscapes of Tanzania.
- Background color: Light, desaturated green (#E8F5E9) to represent the natural environment.
- Accent color: Blue (#90CAF9), similar to the primary but significantly different in saturation and brightness, used for interactive elements and highlights, inspired by the Indian Ocean.
- Headline font: 'Playfair', a modern serif, to convey an elegant and high-end feel. Body font: 'PT Sans', a humanist sans-serif, suitable for body text.
- Use custom icons that reflect the safaris and tours. Icons must be professional and easily understood at a glance.
- Implement a modern grid layout. Content is clearly structured to enhance user experience.
- Subtle transitions and animations for interactive elements such as buttons, image galleries, and form submissions.
